# Contributing to Octoparse MCP Server

Thank you for your interest in improving Octoparse MCP! Here's how you can help.

---

## Reporting Bugs

Please [open a Bug Report](https://github.com/OctoparseData/octoparse-mcp/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) and include:

- What you did
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Your MCP client and OS

---

## Requesting Features

Please [open a Feature Request](https://github.com/OctoparseData/octoparse-mcp/issues/new?template=feature_request.md) and describe:

- The problem you're trying to solve
- Your proposed solution
- Any alternatives you've considered

---

## Questions & Support

For general questions, please use the [Help Center](https://helpcenter.octoparse.com) or email [support@octoparse.com](mailto:support@octoparse.com) rather than opening a GitHub issue.
